• Source: select (or multi-select) box field on which calculation will be based.
  • Title: Text specified by user. Displayed at the top of the graphic.
  • Chart Size: Graphic height and width in pixel .Specified by user . Default (height, width) = (600,800).
  • Pie Size: The pie size relatively to graphic dimension - 0<size<1.
  • On/Off Legend Chart: To enable or disable legend in chart.
  • On/Off Legend table: To enable or disable legend in table.
